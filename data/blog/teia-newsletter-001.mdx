## henland launch

a lot has happened in the last week. on 2/2/22 we started the henland "soft launch" with introducing our new main domain "[henland.xyz](https://henland.xyz/)" and with it a rotating logo: on each site refresh a random logo gets displayed from a pool of submissions. you can find an overview of all the logos on the [logo viewer](https://henland.vercel.app/)

this is supposed to be in place until we decide on a final logo solution, but it is also possible to keep the rotating logos if the community wants that. also: as of now there is no deadline to
[submit new logos via the google form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe27ZughzLlh44BQXjrXto4b9qRTAB9SX-d-YMTCUPYdD6lRg/viewform)

you can find the [launch info article](https://blog.henland.xyz/blog/henland-art-launch-announcement) with a lot of info on the current state of the project and our on our blog.

our next celebratory date will be 22/2/22 - we work on having a few more features ready for that and maybe the community has some ideas for launch events, hastags, celebrations we could use to celebrate the launch. bring your ideas to the #events channel on our discord.

## The new smartcontract

henland uses the same minting contract as hicetnunc. this means OBJKTs minted on henland will show up on all marketplaces that support hen OBJKTs (i.e. objkt.com, rarible, hic.af, etc).
currently we still use the hicetnunc v2 swapping contract. this means that platform fees from collects both on henland and hicetnunc still get directed to the wallet of hicetnunc. in order to collect our own platform fees, [jagracar](https://twitter.com/jagracar) has written and completed a new swapping contract for henland and a multisignature contract that makes it possible to manage the collected fees collectively. Those two new smartcontract have now passed the audit.

[official smartcontract announcement on twitter](https://twitter.com/HenlandCommunity/status/1490285938047651840?s=20&t=kojgwlsJpSGO2fNKhcE05Q)

[a general overview over the new smartcontracts is on our blog](https://blog.henland.xyz/blog/introducing-henc-smart-contracts)

[the full audit report on github](https://github.com/InferenceAG/ReportPublications/blob/master/Inference%20AG%20-%20Henland%20community%20-%20marketplace%20&%20multisig%20-%20v1.0.pdf)

## swapping contract support

with the new smart contract we need to decide how and if we are going to show other swaps (listing prices) of OBJKTs. there are multiple paths we can go: do we show swaps of platforms that show our swaps? do we show all OBJKT swaps and try to position henland as the "platform where you can find all OBJKT prices on one site?” or do we want to only show henland swaps? all these options have certain implications for the usability and positioning of henland in the tez ecosystem.

the communication with objktcom has started on that, probably the same discussions will come up with other platforms soon.

[read and discuss the swap support on discourse ](https://discourse.henland.xyz/t/henland-art-swaps-and-objkt-com/581)

note that this discussion is **not** about showing other tokens minted on other contracts, like tokens minted on objktcom or versum. those are all different minting contracts and we aren't able to display those, at least for now.

**El nuevo contrato inteligente**

_henland_ utiliza el mismo contrato de minteo de Hicetnunc. Esto significa que los OBJKTs acuñados en henland aparecerán en todas las webs que admitan OBJKTs de Hicetnunc (es decir, objkt.com, rarible, hic.af, etc.).

Actualmente todavía utilizando el contrato de swap de Hicetnunc-v2. esto significa que las tarifas que cobra la plataforma tanto en henland como en Hicetnunc todavía se envían a la cartera de Hicetnunc.

Para poder cobrar nuestras propias tarifas en la plataforma, [jagracar](https://twitter.com/jagracar) ha redactado y concluido un nuevo contrato de swap para henland y también un contrato multifirma que permite gestionar las tarifas recaudadas de forma colectiva. Esos dos nuevos contratos inteligentes ya se han aprobado en una auditoría.

[anuncio oficial de contrato inteligente en twitter](https://twitter.com/HenlandCommunity/status/1490285938047651840?s=20&t=kojgwlsJpSGO2fNKhcE05Q)

[descripción general sobre los nuevos contratos inteligentes está en nuestro blog](https://blog.henland.xyz/blog/introducing-henc-smart-contracts)

[el informe de auditoría completo en github](https://github.com/InferenceAG/ReportPublications/blob/master/Inference%20AG%20-%20Henland%20community%20-%20marketplace%20&%20multisig%20-%20v1.0.pdf)

**soporte sobre el contrato de swap**

Con el nuevo contrato inteligente, debemos decidir cómo y si es que vamos a mostrar otros swaps (precios listados) de OBJKTs. Hay varios caminos que podemos tomar: ¿mostramos swaps de plataformas que muestran nuestros swaps? ¿Mostramos todos los swaps de OBJKTs e intentamos posicionar a henland como “La plataforma donde se pueden encontrar todos los precios de OBJKTs en un solo sitio?" ¿O queremos mostrar solo los swaps de henland? Todas estas opciones tienen ciertas implicaciones para la usabilidad y para el posicionamiento de henland dentro del ecosistema de Tezos.

La comunicación en objkt.com ha comenzado ha hablar de eso y probablemente las mismas discusiones surgirán pronto en otras plataformas.

[lee y discute sobre el soporte de swaps en nuestro Discord](https://discourse.henland.xyz/t/henland-art-swaps-and-objkt-com/581)

Toma en cuenta que esta discusión no se trata sobre mostrar otros tokens minteados en otros contratos, como tokens minteados en objkt.com o versum. Todos esos son contratos de minteo diferentes y no podemos mostrarlos, al menos por ahora.
